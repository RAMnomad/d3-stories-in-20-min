D3 Select

                
SVGs aren't like the rest of HTML. 
 
                
Items don't display above, below, and next to each other by default.

                
You have to group items and move them into the position.

                
Each item in sequence within a group is painted on top of the previous one.

                
                    
<html>
                    
<body>
                    
   <svg height=500 width=500>
                    
       <circle r="10" fill="red"></circle>
                    
       <circle r="20" fill="yellow"></circle>
                    
       <circle r="30" fill="green"></circle>
                    
       <circle r="40" fill="blue"></circle>
                    
   </svg>
                    
</html>
